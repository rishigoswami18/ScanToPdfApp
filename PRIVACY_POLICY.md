# Privacy Policy for ScanToPdf

**Last Updated: April 23, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **ScanToPdf** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
*   **Camera Data:** We require access to your device's camera to allow you to scan physical documents. Images captured are processed to generate PDF files.
*   **Storage access:** We require access to your device's storage to save the generated PDF files and to allow you to select existing images for conversion.
*   **Authentication Data:** If you sign in using Google or Email, we collect your basic profile information (name, email) via Firebase Authentication to sync your documents across devices.
*   **Document Data:** Documents you scan may be stored in Firebase Cloud Storage to provide you with cloud backup and multi-device access.

## 2. Use of Information
We use the information collected solely to provide and improve the services of the app, including:
*   Converting images to PDF documents.
*   Performing OCR (Text Recognition) on your scans.
*   Syncing your documents to your account.

## 3. Data Security
We value your trust in providing us your information. We use industry-standard services like Firebase to ensure your data is stored securely. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:
*   [Google Play Services](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)
*   [Google AdMob](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)
*   [Firebase (Cloud Storage, Firestore, Auth)](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
*   [Google ML Kit](https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms)

## 5. Children’s Privacy
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
